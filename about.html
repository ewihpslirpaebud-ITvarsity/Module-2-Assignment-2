ABOUT

PINE CITY MALL is located in one of the wealthiest suburbs in the south of Jo’burg, particularly Aspen Hills, which attracts the affluent resident with a taste for luxury brands. The 72 000 m² regional shopping centre is a retail market leader in mall innovation with an offering of over 160+ fabulous stores, free Wi-Fi and great visibility from two main arterial roads, namely Kliprivier Drive and Swartkoppies Road.
